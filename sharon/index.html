Service/Leadership

  • Heart in Hands Developmental Center, Lincoln NE

    • This center will provide child care for children with special needs. Heart in Hands will be the first child care center for children with special needs in Lincoln, Nebraska. It is currently in progress to apply for certification through the State and collect all the necessary paperwork to open the doors soon. During 2018, I helped organize and coordinate multiple fundraising events, including the collection of raffle tickets from local organizations and businesses to bring in donations needed to establish this child care center.

  • Dancing beyond limits, Lincoln NE

    • During the Fall of 2017, I volunteered as a dancing buddy for the organization ‘Dancing beyond limits’ in Lincoln, Nebraska. The organization creates a dance program for children with special needs, including a few live performances for the parents and friends to attend. The volunteers ensure that the dancers are safe and promote as much independence as possible throughout the dance.

  • Meals on Wheels, Lincoln NE

    • During the Spring of 2016, I helped distribute Meals on Wheels through Tabitha Elder Care in Lincoln, Nebraska for a few weeks. I would have loved to continue this service, but unfortunately, my school schedule did not allow for much flexibility during lunch hours. Meals on Wheels combat senior hunger and isolation through the provision of daily meals (lunch and dinner) to individuals that are homebound or that are unable to financially provide themselves with enough food. The delivery of the meals also provides a well-check for each individual to prevent social isolation for vulnerable seniors.
